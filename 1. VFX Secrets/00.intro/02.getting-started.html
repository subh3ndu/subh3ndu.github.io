Getting Started

This page contains some pointers to help you get support and give us feedback on the course.

We care about your feedback! If you have doubts, get stuck, find a bug, or would like to suggest improvements, please leave a message under the corresponding lesson if you are following the course on Mavenseed.

If not, you can also join our Discord community and use the # courses-support channel to reach us.

While we are careful with our courses and peer review every piece of code and lesson, errors can slip up in a complex project like this one. If you find any, please tell us so we can fix it for everyone! You can use the channels described above or send an email to Nathan: nathan [at] gdquest.com

Downloading the course files

Here is how to download the course files to follow the course offline.

On Gumroad

If you bought the course on Gumroad, download the file that ends in .zip. On your computer, right click on it and select Extract here or a similar option to unpack the zip archive.
